Frequently Asked Questions

The full process typically takes 12 to 18 months from initial application to commencement. Timelines vary based on your country of qualification, document readiness, and processing times for EPIC verification, AMC portfolio assessment, and AHPRA registration. GP Link coordinates each stage to minimise delays.

Helpful?

Yes. We encourage you to explore and apply for positions early through Find My Dream Practice. Securing a placement early helps with visa sponsorship and ensures you have a role lined up when your registration is finalised. Many practices are happy to offer positions to candidates in progress.

Helpful?

Required documents depend on your country of qualification. Generally you will need certified copies of your primary medical degree, specialist qualification certificates (e.g. MRCGP, MICGP, FRNZCGP), a current CV, certificate of good standing, and proof of training completion. Each step in The Journey will guide you through exactly what is needed.

Helpful?

Once you secure a practice placement, your employer will act as your visa sponsor. GP Link works with both you and the practice to prepare the nomination, lodge your visa application, and track it through to grant. The Visa Application step in The Journey provides full visibility into each stage of this process.

Helpful?

GP Link manages your entire pathway from start to finish. We handle document verification, coordinate with EPIC, AMC and AHPRA, match you with practices, support your visa application, and prepare you for commencement. Our team responds during business hours and you can reach us anytime through the Messages tab.

Helpful?
We're Here For You! Our dedicated support team understands the complexities of GP registration. We respond during business hours (Mon-Fri, 9am-5pm AEST). We aim to respond within 1 hour.
