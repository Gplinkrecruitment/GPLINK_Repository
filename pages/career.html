How to apply

GP Link keeps the early process confidential and only releases practice identity after the practice accepts your application.

Step 1 Open a role, review the summary, suburb and benefits, then save it if you want to return later.
Step 2 Apply through GP Link so your profile, documents and preferences can be checked before submission.
Step 3 Once the practice accepts your application, GP Link releases the practice identity and coordinates the next conversation.
